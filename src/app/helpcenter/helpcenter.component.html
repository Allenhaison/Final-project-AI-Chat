What is EchoAI?

Commonly asked questions about EchoAI

How much does it cost to use EchoAI?

EchoAI is free to use, though we do have subscription plans available, available on our pricing page.

How does EchoAI work?

EchoAI is based on the GPT-3 architecture, which uses deep learning techniques to generate human-like text responses. It has been trained on a diverse range of internet text, allowing it to understand and generate text in a conversational manner.

Why does the AI seem so real and lifelike?

EchoAI's ability to generate human-like text is a result of its training on vast amounts of text data and its underlying architecture, which is designed to understand context and produce coherent responses.

Can I trust that the AI is telling me the truth?

While EchoAI strives for accuracy, it may occasionally produce incorrect or misleading information. It's essential to verify critical information from reliable sources.

Who can view my conversations?

Your conversations with EchoAI are not visible to others. However, it's important to avoid sharing sensitive personal information during your interactions.

Will you use my conversations for training?

We may use your conversations for research and development purposes, including improving our models. However, we take privacy seriously and will not share your personal information.

How can I provide feedback?

You can provide feedback through our feedback form, which is accessible from the main menu. We appreciate your input!
